Instructions
  • Your goal is to fill in the grid to form a picture.
  • Left-click to fill in a square.
  • Right-click to mark a square as empty.
  • Unsure? Use middle-click to mark a square as a maybe!
  • You can also use the buttons below the grid to change mouse modes. The X turns left-click into X-Mode, while the ? turns left-click into Maybe-Mode. The cursor returns the mouse-mode to normal.
  • The numbers on the side and top tell you how many squares in that row or column are filled in.
  • Numbers separated by a space mean that there is at least one empty square between the sets of filled in blocks.

*hint* If the number is the same size as the grid, the whole row or column can be filled in!
